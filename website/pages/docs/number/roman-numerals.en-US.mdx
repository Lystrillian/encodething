## Roman Numerals

Roman Numerals are a numeral system that originated in ancient Rome and remained the standard way of writing numbers throughout Europe well into the Late Middle Ages. Encodething allows you to convert between Roman Numerals and Arabic numerals (base 10) with ease.

Encodething supports Roman Numeral conversions within the range of 1 to 3999. Values outside this range will result in an error, as the Roman Numeral system does not have consistent representations for larger numbers.
