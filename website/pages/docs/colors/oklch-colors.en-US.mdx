## About OKLCH Colors

OKLCH Colors are a color space that uses a combination of opacity, lightness, chroma, and hue to represent colors. It is a more intuitive and versatile alternative to RGB, which uses red, green, and blue values to represent colors.

OKLCH colors are often used in web design and programming to create more visually appealing and dynamic color schemes. They are also commonly used in graphic design, photography, and other visual arts.
